# CRM Evaluation Overview

## Temple Foundation (Working Name)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Organization Overview

**Temple Foundation (working name)** is a small, emerging nonprofit
organization based in Spokane, Washington. The organization's mission
centers on fostering wellbeing through spirituality, personal growth,
and community connection. Planned activities include community
gatherings, spiritual education events, workshops, and other programming
designed to cultivate meaningful engagement and shared development.

The organization is in its formative stage and is currently establishing
foundational operational systems. A key priority is selecting and
implementing a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform to
support event coordination, donor management, communications, and
long-term organizational growth.

This repository documents the structured research, evaluation criteria,
and final recommendation process for selecting a CRM solution that
aligns with both immediate operational needs and anticipated future
expansion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Current Operational Context

-   Early-stage nonprofit
-   Limited administrative staff
-   Cost-sensitive (target budget: \$50--\$100/month)
-   Anticipated need for event management and community engagement tools
-   Website platform not yet determined
-   Desire for future website integration and system scalability

The selected CRM must balance affordability, simplicity, and long-term
adaptability without introducing unnecessary technical overhead.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Anticipated Functional Requirements

While exact workflows are still being refined, the CRM solution is
expected to support:

-   Contact management (participants, donors, volunteers)
-   Event registration and attendance tracking
-   Online donation processing (one-time and recurring)
-   Email communication and segmentation
-   Basic reporting and engagement insights
-   Integration capability with a future website (API, embed, or
    plugin-based)

As the organization grows, additional requirements may include:

-   Membership management
-   Volunteer coordination
-   Advanced donor reporting
-   Automation workflows
-   Multi-user administrative roles

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Project Scope

Kelly (Owner/Founder) has commissioned independent research and
evaluation to identify a CRM platform that:

1.  Meets present operational needs.\
2.  Remains cost-effective within the defined monthly budget.\
3.  Scales with the organization's growth.\
4.  Integrates cleanly with a future website architecture.\
5.  Minimizes long-term technical maintenance burden.

This repository serves as a living documentation source for:

-   Requirements gathering\
-   Vendor comparison\
-   Cost modeling\
-   Risk assessment\
-   Final recommendation rationale
