# Potential CRM Capabilities

This is a nonprofit-focused list of CRM capabilities to consider for current and future evaluation needs (including some features that may not be immediately required)

## Core Contact & Database Features

1. Contact records
   - Individual profiles with core identity and engagement details.
2. Organization/company records
   - Profiles for businesses, partners, and institutional donors.
3. Household records
   - Group related contacts under shared household relationships.
4. Custom fields
   - Add nonprofit-specific data fields beyond default CRM fields.
5. Tagging
   - Apply flexible labels for quick categorization and filtering.
6. Segmentation
   - Build targeted lists based on attributes, behavior, or history.
7. Lead status tracking
   - Track inquiry-to-supporter progression through defined stages.
8. Relationship mapping
   - Record relationships between people, households, and organizations.
9. Notes on records
   - Store contextual notes from calls, meetings, and interactions.
10. File attachments
    - Attach documents such as forms, letters, and agreements.
11. Contact timelines
    - View chronological history of all activity for a record.
12. Interaction history
    - Log emails, calls, meetings, and other touchpoints.
13. Activity tracking
    - Track tasks, reminders, and completed follow-up actions.
14. Call logging
    - Record call outcomes, summaries, and next steps.
15. SMS logging
    - Track text-message outreach and responses per contact.
16. Email logging
    - Capture outbound/inbound email activity linked to records.
17. Social media profile linking
    - Associate public social profiles to contact records.
18. Contact merging
    - Combine duplicate records while preserving key data.
19. Duplicate detection
    - Automatically flag potential duplicate records for review.
20. Data import/export (CSV)
    - Move data in/out using common CSV format.
21. Constituent self-service portal (profile + preferences)
    - Let constituents update their own details and communication preferences.
22. Householding automation rules
    - Automatically assign and maintain household groupings.
23. Communication consent history tracking
    - Keep auditable opt-in/opt-out consent records.
24. Field-level permissions
    - Restrict sensitive fields by user role.
25. Audit logs for record changes
    - Track who changed what and when.
26. Data retention and archival controls
    - Apply retention rules and archive inactive data.
27. Real-time duplicate prevention on form entry/import
    - Warn users of likely duplicates before save/import.

## Donations & Fundraising

1. One-time donations
   - Accept and record single gifts from supporters.
2. Recurring donations
   - Set up and manage scheduled ongoing contributions.
3. Donation forms (embeddable)
   - Embed branded giving forms on website pages.
4. Donation page builder
   - Create standalone donation pages without custom coding.
5. Peer-to-peer fundraising
   - Enable supporters to fundraise on behalf of the organization.
6. Campaign tracking
   - Monitor gifts and performance by fundraising campaign.
7. Pledge tracking
   - Record pledged amounts and track payment schedules.
8. Tribute donations
   - Capture gifts made in honor or memory of someone.
9. Employer gift matching tracking
   - Track matching-gift eligibility and submission progress.
10. Donation receipts (auto-generated)
    - Automatically issue receipts after completed gifts.
11. Tax receipt generation
    - Generate compliant year-end or per-gift tax statements.
12. Offline donation entry
    - Enter cash, check, and other non-digital gifts.
13. Fund allocation tracking
    - Assign donations to specific funds or programs.
14. Grant tracking
    - Track grant opportunities, statuses, and awarded amounts.
15. Major donor tracking
    - Manage high-capacity donor relationships and activities.
16. Donor tiers
    - Classify supporters by giving level thresholds.
17. Giving history timeline
    - View chronological donation history per constituent.
18. Donor lifetime value calculation
    - Estimate total long-term value of each donor.
19. Wealth screening integrations
    - Connect to tools that identify donor capacity indicators.
20. Donor segmentation
    - Group donors for targeted stewardship and appeals.
21. Soft credits and gift attribution tracking
    - Attribute credit to influencers, households, or related contacts.
22. Matching gift workflow automation
    - Automate reminders and follow-up for match submissions.
23. Recurring gift failed-payment recovery workflows
    - Trigger retries, alerts, and outreach after payment failures.
24. Major gift moves management pipeline
    - Track cultivation stages, asks, and next actions.
25. Planned giving / legacy gift tracking
    - Record and steward bequests and planned gift intentions.
26. Grant deadline and reporting workflow reminders
    - Set reminders for grant submissions and required reports.
27. Donation payout reconciliation
    - Reconcile processor payouts with recorded gift transactions.

## Event & RSVP Management

1. Event creation
   - Set up event records with dates, locations, and event details.
2. Event landing pages
   - Publish branded pages with event information and registration links.
3. Event ticketing
   - Sell and manage ticket types for paid events.
4. Tiered ticket pricing
   - Offer multiple ticket levels with different prices or benefits.
5. Early bird pricing
   - Apply discounted pricing for early registrations.
6. RSVP tracking
   - Record attendee responses and registration status.
7. Guest list management
   - Manage attendee rosters, statuses, and check-in readiness.
8. Check-in tools
   - Verify attendance on-site during event entry.
9. QR code ticketing
   - Generate and scan QR-based tickets for faster entry.
10. Waitlist management
    - Maintain waitlists and promote attendees as spots open.
11. Table assignment
    - Assign attendees to tables or seating groups.
12. Sponsorship tracking
    - Track sponsor commitments, benefits, and fulfillment status.
13. Event capacity limits
    - Enforce registration caps to prevent overbooking.
14. Event reminder automation
    - Send timed reminders before events automatically.
15. Event feedback surveys
    - Collect post-event feedback from attendees.
16. Attendance tracking on contact record
    - Store each contact’s attendance history for future segmentation.
17. Event revenue tracking
    - Report ticket, donation, and sponsorship revenue by event.
18. Virtual event integrations
    - Connect events with webinar or livestream platforms.
19. Multi-session event support
    - Manage events with multiple sessions or breakout tracks.
20. Event analytics reporting
    - Analyze registration, attendance, and conversion metrics.
21. Mobile event check-in (phone/tablet)
    - Check in attendees using mobile devices on-site.
22. Offline check-in with sync
    - Continue check-in without internet and sync later.
23. Automated post-event follow-up workflows
    - Trigger thank-yous, surveys, and next-step outreach after events.

2. Volunteer shift scheduling

3. Volunteer hours tracking
   - Record volunteer time served for reporting and recognition.
4. Skills tracking

5. Background check tracking

6. Membership levels
   - Define and manage tiered membership categories.
7. Membership renewal reminders
   - Send automated reminders before membership expiration.
8. Membership dues processing
   - Collect and record membership payments and statuses.
9. Certification tracking
   - Track required certifications and expiration dates.
10. Volunteer engagement history
    - View each volunteer’s participation and interaction timeline.
11. Volunteer self-service portal
    - Let volunteers manage profiles, availability, and preferences.
12. Volunteer hour approval workflows
    - Route submitted hours for review and approval.
13. Volunteer onboarding checklist workflows
    - Standardize onboarding tasks and completion tracking.

## Email & Marketing Automation

1. Email campaign builder
   - Create and send one-time or scheduled campaign emails.
2. Email templates
   - Reuse branded templates for consistent communications.
3. Automated welcome sequences
   - Trigger onboarding emails after sign-up or first donation.
4. Drip campaigns
   - Send timed multi-step nurture sequences over time.
5. Behavior-based automation
   - Trigger messaging based on actions like opens, clicks, or registrations.
6. Triggered emails
   - Send event-based emails automatically from specific CRM events.
7. A/B testing
   - Test subject lines or content variants to improve performance.
8. Email open tracking
   - Measure open rates for campaign effectiveness.
9. Click tracking
   - Track link clicks to understand engagement intent.
10. Unsubscribe management
    - Manage opt-outs and suppression lists automatically.
11. SMS marketing
    - Send text-message campaigns and alerts to opted-in contacts.
12. Landing page builder
    - Build campaign landing pages without custom development.
13. Pop-up form builder
    - Create on-site popup forms for signups and lead capture.
14. Newsletter management
    - Manage recurring newsletter production and distribution.
15. Marketing attribution tracking
    - Attribute outcomes to channels, campaigns, and touchpoints.
16. Multilingual email templates
    - Support communication templates in multiple languages.
17. Journey-based communication builder
    - Design branching lifecycle journeys across constituent segments.
18. Preference-center integration for subscriptions
    - Let contacts manage topic and channel subscription preferences.

## Reporting & Analytics

1. Custom dashboards
   - Build role-specific dashboards for key metrics at a glance.
2. Revenue reporting
   - Report income by campaign, source, date range, and fund.
3. Campaign ROI tracking
   - Compare campaign costs against fundraising returns.
4. Donor retention rate reporting
   - Measure year-over-year donor retention and churn.
5. Funnel reporting
   - Track conversion stages from prospect to donor.
6. Forecasting
   - Project revenue and participation using historical trends.
7. Volunteer activity reports
   - Summarize volunteer hours, participation, and engagement levels.
8. Event performance reports
   - Analyze registrations, attendance, and event outcomes.
9. Data filtering & custom reports
   - Create ad hoc reports with flexible filters and fields.
10. Exportable PDF reports
    - Export formatted reports for sharing with stakeholders.
11. Goal pacing dashboards (actual vs target)
    - Show progress toward fundraising and engagement goals over time.
12. Board-ready KPI report packs
    - Compile executive-ready KPI summaries for board meetings.
13. Data quality dashboards (missing/stale/invalid records)
    - Monitor record completeness and data hygiene issues.
14. Major gift pipeline and stage velocity reporting
    - Report major-donor pipeline health and movement speed by stage.

## Integrations & System Features

1. Stripe integration
   - Connect Stripe for online payment processing and gift capture.
2. PayPal integration
   - Accept and reconcile donations processed through PayPal.
3. QuickBooks integration
   - Sync financial transactions to accounting workflows.
4. Zapier / automation tool integration
   - Connect CRM actions to external tools using no-code automations.
5. API access
   - Programmatically read/write data for custom integrations.
6. WordPress/CMS integration
   - Embed forms and connect website content workflows.
7. Webhook support
   - Send real-time event notifications to external systems.
8. Scheduled accounting sync and export mappings
   - Automate periodic exports and map fields for finance systems.
9. Single sign-on (SSO)
   - Centralize authentication and simplify secure user access.
